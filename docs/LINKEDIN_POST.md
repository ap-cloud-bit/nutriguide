🥦 I built NutriGuide — an AI Food Intelligence Agent from scratch.

Here's what it does in 30 seconds:

📷 Scan any food barcode → get a real-time health score (0–10), UPF rating, and AI analysis
📋 Generate a 7-day meal plan based on YOUR budget, goals, and what's already in your fridge
🛒 Optimize your shopping cart for cost AND nutrition
💬 Chat with an AI nutritionist — powered by Claude

---

The tech stack I used:
→ FastAPI (Python) for the backend API
→ Open Food Facts (6M+ products, 100% free)
→ Anthropic Claude for AI intelligence
→ Deployed on Railway + Vercel (free tiers)
→ Docker for one-command local setup

---

The insight that started this: PwC's 2025 Consumer Report found that 73% of people struggle to interpret food labels, and UPF consumption keeps rising despite 68% of people being concerned about it.

There's a massive gap between what people want to eat and what they actually buy. AI can close that gap.
